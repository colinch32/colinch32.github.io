제티 15개, 설탕 60g, 옥수수 전분가루 25g, 우유 500g, 생크림 50g

![](https://img1.daumcdn.net/thumb/R720x0/?fname=https%3A%2F%2Ft1.daumcdn.net%2Fliveboard%2Fdispatch%2F22fe283cebc849de9ebb09352eae415e.JPG)
